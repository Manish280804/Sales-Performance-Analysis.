Sales Performance Analysis

Overview
This project demonstrates a "hybrid analytics workflow" using "SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI".  
It analyzes an E-commerce Orders dataset to uncover insights about "sales, profit, regions, categories, and top products".  
The goal is to showcase "end-to-end data analysis skills" in a recruiter-friendly format.

Tools & Technologies
- "SQL" - Data import, cleaning, and core queries  
- "Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, SQLAlchemy)" - Advanced analysis & visualizations  
- "Power BI" - Interactive dashboard with KPIs & charts  
- "Excel" - Initial dataset exploration  

Key Insights
- "Total Sales & Profit" across dataset  
- "Monthly Sales Trend" (line chart)  
- "Profit by Category" (bar chart)  
- "Sales by Region" (pie chart)  
- "Top 10 Products by Sales" (bar chart)  

Business Insights
- "Monthly Sales Trend" shows steady growth with clear seasonal spikes in November–December (holiday season impact).  
- "Electronics category" contributes the highest share of profit margins compared to other categories.  
- "North Region" drives the largest portion of sales, while South Region shows potential for growth.  
- "Top 10 products" together account for nearly one‑third of total sales, highlighting strong product concentration.  
- Certain categories show declining profit despite stable sales, indicating need for cost optimization.  
- Average order value remains consistent, suggesting stable customer purchasing behavior across months.
